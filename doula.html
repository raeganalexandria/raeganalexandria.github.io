Current Postpartum Doula Offerings

Doulas are skilled helpers around the event of birth, who have existed since humans have had babies in community. They are familiar with the territory, and contribute effectively in the ways unique to each person and family. It is a collaborative effort to help identify, establish and integrate flow in the home. I envision a return to past traditions where assistance was inherent to the experience of being in a family system or a community. Until that time comes, and in order for it to do so, we must actively call upon others for care. I see this as both the most vulnerable and strong thing a human can do. My intention is to help facilitate an empowered, integrated and whole postpartum journey.

Services

These will vary depending on what is needed. From spiritual and emotional support in the form of private yoga, ceremony, or home cleansing, to practical daily life realities like loading the dishwasher. We will work together to create a plan that fits your individual needs. The beauty of this service is that I am prepared to step into the dynamic situation and hold you and your family in a way which is appropriate and helpful.

The Running List (including but not limited to)

  • Preparing organic, homemade, nourishing meals appropriate for the recovery from birth. I follow the traditional philosophy of warming, nutrient dense, healing foods. This can be delivered or prepared in your home.
  • Providing infant care so that one or both parents may sleep, shower, run an errand, etc.
  • Light cleaning and energetic cleansing of the home.
  • Tidying the main living spaces, re-stocking the fridge, running a load of laundry.
  • Drawing a restorative bath.
  • Walking dogs.
  • Caring for other children. (Note: This is not a full time nanny offering)
  • Sanitizing, freezing, thawing, bottling, warming milk. I am not currently a certified lactation consultant.
